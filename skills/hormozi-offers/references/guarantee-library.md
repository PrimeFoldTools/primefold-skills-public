# Guarantee Library

Pre-built guarantee templates for different product types and business models.

---

## How to Use

1. Find your product type
2. Choose guarantee level
3. Customize with specifics
4. Track refund rates
5. Adjust as needed

---

## Template Types

### Digital Products

**Unconditional 30-Day (8-12% refund rate)**
"Try completely risk-free for 30 days. Not satisfied? Full refund, no questions asked. Keep bonuses."

**Conditional Completion (3-6% refund rate)**
"Complete [milestone] within [timeframe]. Not satisfied? Show us work, get full refund + keep materials."

**Results-Based (Variable, +60-100% conversion)**
"Achieve [result] within [timeframe] or we'll [work until you do / refund]."

---

### SaaS/Software

**Extended Free Trial (5-8% refund)**
"Try free for X days. Cancel anytime. Continue but unsatisfied? Prorated refund."

**Feature-Specific (2-5% refund)**
"Use [feature] for [time]. Doesn't deliver [benefit]? Refund + transition help."

---

### Coaching/Services

**Outcome or Keep Working (0% refund, higher conversion)**
"Work together for [time]. Don't achieve [milestone]? Continue free until you do."

**Hybrid Service (2-4% refund, +45-65% conversion)**
"Get [deliverables] on schedule. Don't? [Free month / partial refund]. Achieve outcome or refund balance."

---

## Guarantee Strength Ladder

1. **Standard Return** - Return within X days
2. **Money-Back** - Do X, if not satisfied, refund
3. **Unconditional** - Satisfied for any reason or refund
4. **Results-Based** - Achieve X or refund
5. **Better-Than-Money-Back** - Refund + bonus
6. **Outcome or Keep Working** - Work until achievement
7. **Pay-For-Performance** - Only pay if results (rarest)

---

## Refund Rate Benchmarks

- <2%: Guarantee too weak
- 2-8%: Sweet spot
- 8-15%: Normal for unconditional
- >15%: Quality issue or wrong targeting
