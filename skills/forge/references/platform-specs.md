# Platform Specifications & Constraints

Verified constraints and optimization guidance for major content platforms.

---

## TikTok
- **Aspect ratio:** 9:16 (vertical)
- **Hook window:** 1-3 seconds
- **Optimal length:** 21-34s for scroll
- **Algorithm priority:** Watch time > completion > engagement
- **Behavior:** Suppress non-native content with watermarks

## Instagram
- **Reels:** 9:16, 15-90s, 1.5-3s hook
- **Feed:** 1:1 or 4:5 aspect
- **Stories:** 9:16, 15s per story
- **Algorithm:** Shares > saves > comments > likes
- **Behavior:** Penalizes re-uploaded TikToks

## LinkedIn
- **Text posts:** 3000 characters
- **Hook:** First 2 lines critical (140 chars)
- **Videos:** 1:1 or 16:9, up to 10min
- **Algorithm:** Dwell time > comments > shares
- **Tone:** Professional required

## X (Twitter)
- **Text:** 280 chars (4000 for Premium)
- **Videos:** Up to 2:20 (10min Premium)
- **Algorithm:** Engagement velocity matters
- **Behavior:** Hashtags 1-2 max (excessive = spam)

## YouTube
- **Long-form:** 16:9, optimal 8-12min
- **Shorts:** 9:16, up to 60s, 1-2s hook
- **Algorithm:** CTR × watch time
- **Behavior:** First 24-48hrs critical

## Pinterest
- **Aspect ratio:** 2:3 vertical (1000×1500px)
- **Algorithm:** Saves + outbound clicks
- **Content:** Evergreen, search-driven
- **Behavior:** Quality > quantity

---

**Remember:** Native uploads outperform cross-posts. Platform-specific adaptation is non-negotiable.
