# Hook Library

Comprehensive hook archetypes organized by intent and discovery mode.

---

## Selection Framework
1. Identify primary intent
2. Identify discovery mode (scroll vs. search)
3. Select matching archetype
4. Adapt to platform constraints

---

## Discovery Intent Hooks

### Scroll/Feed Mode
- Pattern Interrupt + Curiosity Gap: "Nobody talks about this..."
- Contradiction: "Everyone says X. The opposite is true."
- Emotional Amplification: "Tired of X? Me too. Here's why..."
- Number/List Promise: "3 things I wish I knew..."

### Search Mode
- Problem + Solution: "How to [X] without [pain point]"
- Authority Signal: "After [number] years, here's..."

---

## Trust Intent Hooks

### Scroll/Feed Mode
- Behind-the-Scenes: "Here's what really happens..."
- Vulnerability Share: "I messed this up. Here's what I learned..."
- Value-First Teaching: "Here's exactly how to..."

### Search Mode
- Step-by-Step Guide: "Complete guide to [X]"
- Resource Compilation: "Top 10 [tools] for [purpose]"

---

## Conversion Intent Hooks

### Scroll/Feed Mode
- Transformation Promise: "From [bad] to [good] in [timeframe]"
- Scarcity/Urgency: "Last chance to [X]..."
- Problem Agitation: "Still struggling with [X]? Here's why..."

### Search Mode
- Specific Outcome Promise: "Get [specific result] using [method]"
- Comparison/Alternative: "[X] vs. [Y]: Which is better?"

---

## Retention Intent Hooks
- Series Setup: "Part 1: The beginning of..."
- Cliffhanger/Continuation: "Wait until you see what happens..."
- Community Callback: "Remember when we talked about [X]?"

---

## Authority Intent Hooks
- Contrarian Insight: "Everyone's wrong about [X]. Here's why..."
- Original Framework: "Introducing the [X] framework..."
- Industry Critique: "Why [industry practice] is broken..."

---

**Remember:** Hooks are intent-specific, mode-specific, and platform-adapted. No universal hook works everywhere.
